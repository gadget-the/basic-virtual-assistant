speech recognition?
    what I really want in the way of speech recognition is being able to talk to it in a very natural way, like you would with another person
        I would want it to be able to discern when it's being talked to
        be able to just start talking to it in a certain tone, and it pick that up

        started thinking about this because I thought about Alexa/Amazon Echo and the wake word "Alexa"
            that seems like a pretty natural way to get someone's attention
            I started thinking about how it would know if you were talking to it when you say the wake word in different contexts/tones/whatever

        this is really something that would be well handled by machine learning(i.e. a neural network)
            train it on different audio clips of people talking in different ways
            have it learn when someone is talking to it and not

        the current system just listens to live audio and waits until it hears a wake word/phrase
            this makes difficult to have a conversation with the thing, which was a goal I kinda had
                "Hey Assistant, How are you?", "Hey Assistant, What did you do today?", "Hey Assistant, I'm doing good, what about you?"...
            
            of course, people do tend to respond to their names(and nicknames, and other titles, etc.), which is a clear parallel for the wake phrase thing
                but it would be kinda weird if everytime you were talking with someone you had to keep calling out their name to keep their attention
                    "Hey Jerry, Good morning!", "Jerry, what did you do today?", "Hey Jerry, I do like that idea."...
